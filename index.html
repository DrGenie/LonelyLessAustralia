LonelyLessAustralia Decision Aid Tool

Loneliness is increasingly acknowledged as a major issue affecting older adults in Australia, resulting in significant health, social, and economic challenges. This tool showcases how various programme features—cost, delivery method, and more—can shape older adults’ uptake preferences, building on discrete choice experiment (DCE) findings.

Use the tabs above to navigate:

  • Introduction: This overview page.
  • Inputs: Configure or select attributes for your scenario.
  • Scenarios: Save or compare multiple scenarios side by side.

After selecting attributes in the Inputs tab, you can generate predicted probabilities or cost-benefit outputs in a new window, download PDF reports, and explore how preferences differ by loneliness category (Not Lonely, Moderately Lonely, Severely Lonely).
