LonelyLessAustralia Decision Aid Tool

Loneliness has been recognised as a major concern among older adults in Australia, contributing to adverse health, social, and economic consequences. This tool demonstrates how key programme attributes—from cost levels to service delivery methods—can influence uptake decisions, drawing on insights from discrete choice experiments (DCEs).

Use the menu above to navigate:

  • Introduction – this overview page.
  • Inputs – select or configure attributes for your scenario.
  • Scenarios – view and compare your saved scenarios.

Once you have selected attributes in the Inputs tab, you can generate predicted probabilities or cost-benefit estimates in a new window, download PDF reports, and explore how preferences differ by loneliness category.
